AMBAR Dating App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

1. Introduction

AMBAR Dating App (“we,” “our,” “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our app. By using AMBAR Dating App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Age Restriction

AMBAR Dating App is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly allow underage users to register or use our services.

3. Data We Collect

  • Basic Account Information: UID, email, name, age, gender, country, profile images
  • Profile & Preferences: Height, partner type, children, bio, social media status
  • Lifestyle & Beliefs: Religion, political belief, education, drink/smoke preferences, communication style, jealousy, family attachment, personality type, feelings, marriage history, marriage year plan, previous, date outside religion, night club frequency
  • Location Data: Precise location, country
  • App Activity & Features: Likes, matches, chats, posts, comments, reports, points, superChat
  • Verification & Security: Profile Verification, referral codes, FCM tokens (for push notifications)

4. Data Storage

All user data is stored securely on our VPS servers. We do not store sensitive personal data locally on devices beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide account access and authentication (Google Sign-In) via Firebase.
  • Enable app features such as posting stories, liking, commenting, editing, deleting, reporting, creating matches, and chatting.
  • Personalize matchmaking through our algorithm.
  • Send notifications related to app activity.
  • Moderate and maintain a safe community environment.
  • Display test ads via AdMob (non-personalized).

6. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties for marketing or analytics. Third-party services used include:

  • Google Sign-In (account login) via Firebase Auth
  • AdMob (test ads only, non-personalized)
  • No payment processors are currently integrated

7. User Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. Essential personal information will be deleted within one month of request.
  • Control: Manage your posts, likes, comments, matches, and chats directly within the app.

8. Security Measures

  • Encryption of user photos, messages, and account information.
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth (Google Sign-In).
  • Manual and automated moderation to prevent misuse.

9. Community Guidelines & Safety

We enforce strict rules to maintain a safe environment: zero tolerance for harassment, abuse, or illegal activity. Reporting tools are available for inappropriate content or behavior. Moderation actions may include content removal, account suspension, or cooperation with law enforcement.

10. Ads & Premium Features

Currently, only test ads via AdMob are displayed. No payments are processed at this stage; premium features are free during closed testing. Future updates may introduce payments, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

11. Continuous Improvement

We are committed to improving safety and privacy. Feedback from users is welcome and will help us refine our policies and practices.
